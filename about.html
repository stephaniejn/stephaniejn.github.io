Who is Stephanie?

Stephanie is an aspiring web developer with skills and experience in marketing, social media and film production. She attended Queen's University in Eastern Ontario and studied abroad for two semesters in England and Denmark.

After graduating, Stephanie worked for a year in Toronto and then made her way back to the west coast, spending six months in Vancouver and moving back to Seattle in December 2013. She couldn't be happier to be back in the land of mountains, rain and excellent sushi. Stephanie is passionate about travel, hiking, photography and taekwondo.
